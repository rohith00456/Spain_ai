FAQ

Got Questions?

How does SpineAI detect poor posture? +

SpineAI uses an MPU-6050 inertial measurement unit to measure your spine's tilt angle in real-time. When you slouch beyond your threshold for more than 30 seconds, it triggers a gentle vibration.

Is the device comfortable? +

Absolutely. SpineAI weighs just 12 grams — lighter than two coins. It's designed for all-day comfort and is virtually invisible under clothing.

How long does the battery last and how do I charge it? +

SpineAI features an ultra-low power design that gives you up to 14 days of battery life on a single charge. It charges fully in under 2 hours via a standard USB-C port.

Can I use it while working out or running? +

While SpineAI is optimized for desk posture tracking, it can be worn during light physical activities. You can temporarily pause alerts through the companion app to prevent vibrations during active workouts.

Does the device work offline without the companion app? +

Yes, absolutely. SpineAI performs all posture classification and haptic correction alerts using its built-in Edge AI chip. It will sync your posture history to the app once it reconnects via Bluetooth.
